Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2023

At Spray Aqua Resort, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we handle your information when you use our booking system.

1. Information We Collect

We collect basic information necessary for park entry and booking confirmation, including:

  • Full Name
  • Mobile Number (for WhatsApp tickets and verification)
  • Visit Date and Time Preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data solely for:

  • Processing your ticket booking.
  • Sending official entry passes and QR codes via WhatsApp API.
  • Managing park capacity and security.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Your information is only shared with:

  • Meta (WhatsApp): To deliver your digital tickets.
  • Google Cloud: To store booking records for our internal management.

4. Your Rights

You have the right to ask about the data we hold about your booking or request deletion after your visit is completed.

5. Security

We use industry-standard encryption and secure cloud services (Vercel, Google Cloud) to protect your personal information.
